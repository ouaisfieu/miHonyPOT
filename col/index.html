A Diversified Grid

While fossil fuels will not disappear by 2030, their share in the electricity mix is shrinking rapidly. The future grid is a hybrid ecosystem dominated by variable renewables supported by firm dispatchable power and storage.

  • Solar PV: Fastest Growth
  • Offshore Wind: High Capacity Factor
  • Fossil Fuels: Declining Share
